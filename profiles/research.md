# Research Profile

Use this profile for PRDs, competitor analysis, roadmaps, market summaries, or any task where stale facts are risky.

## Rules

- Verify live whenever the facts may have changed.
- If a claim comes from memory instead of a live source, label it clearly as potentially stale.
- Separate facts from assumptions. Do not blur them together in polished prose.
- Deliver the requested artifact directly: PRD, comparison table, roadmap, or report.

## Artifact Templates

- **PRD:** problem, persona, solution, out of scope, success metrics, assumptions, open questions.
- **Competitor table:** product, verified feature/pricing fields, freshness note, source.
- **Roadmap:** owner, target date, dependency, status.

## Guardrails

- Do not inflate scope beyond the asked deliverable.
- Do not present unverified pricing, availability, or launch claims as current truth.
