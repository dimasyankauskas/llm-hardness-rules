**Problem Statement**
Users frequently order from a small set of preferred restaurants but must navigate a crowded marketplace or search history each time. This repetition causes decision fatigue and increases the time-to-order, creating friction for high-intent, returning customers.

**Target Persona**
**The Busy Regular:** A time-constrained professional who orders lunch or dinner 3–5 times per week. They have 2–3 "go-to" restaurants and prioritize a fast, frictionless path to checkout over discovering new cuisines.

**Core User Flow**
1. **Tagging:** User taps the "Star" icon on a restaurant profile or a previous order to add it to their Favorites.
2. **Accessing:** User opens the "Favorites" tab via the primary navigation bar.
3. **Selection:** User selects a starred restaurant from the curated list.
4. **Quick Reorder:** User taps "Reorder Last Items" to instantly populate their cart with their most recent order from that restaurant.
5. **Checkout:** User confirms the order and completes payment.

**Success Metrics**
1. **Conversion Velocity:** Reduce the average time from app launch to checkout by 25% for users ordering via the Favorites tab compared to the general search flow.
2. **Feature Adoption:** 40% of users who order more than twice a month "Star" at least one restaurant within the first 30 days of release.
3. **Repeat Order Rate:** A 15% increase in month-over-month order frequency for users who have at least 3 restaurants in their Favorites list.
